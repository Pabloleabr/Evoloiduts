A deck building roguelike where you will combine your cards with new ones you find, obliterating enemies with the cards that you make and becoming more powerful with each card you fuse.

Features:

  • Strategic Deckbuilding: You will need to think ahead on what cards to combine and find what abilities work best with each other. Think carefully of each ability you add to your card or let chaos take over you and get many different abilities on your cards!
  • Define your build: While fighting enemies you will gain XP that will allow you to unlock special skills. These skills will let you focus your build on any direction you wish to take it.
  • Randomly generated paths: Go through a new map each game that is generated from a predetermined variety of enemies and events. Find rare enemies or cards that will make your run stand out from others.
